General Page layout
The paper size is 8.5" x 11" with 0.83" top margins, 0.25" bottom margins, 0.5" inside margins, and 0.42" outside margins.
The Double Columns have widths 3.39" and 0.17" spacing
Headers have The Journal name and manuscript ID on the left, and the page number on the right (first page included)

Content Formatting
Times New Roman 10pt. First headers are centered and are all-caps while second level headers are justified left and in italics.
The body font is 9.5 Times New Roman. Links use Arial and
Images and Graphics must have captions written in 8pt Helvetica. Equations are written in Latex, rendered, then inserted as an image.
