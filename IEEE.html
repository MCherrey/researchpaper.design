General Page layout

Margins

Use 8.5" x 11" paper with 0.83" top margins, 0.25" bottom margins, 0.5" inside margins, and 0.42" outside margins.

Columns

The Double Columns have widths 3.39" and 0.17" spacing

Headers

Headers have The Journal name and manuscript ID on the left, and the page number on the right (first page included)

Fonts

Headings

Times New Roman 10pt. First headers are centered and are all-caps while second level headers are justified left and in italics.

Body

The body font is 9.5 Times New Roman. Links are in blue and underlined. The abstract is in 9t and is bolded.

Images and Graphics

Images and Graphics must have captions written with 8pt Times New Roman. Equations are written in Latex, rendered, then inserted as an image.

Citations

Order

Citations are listed in order of appearance in the document.

Citation Format

Citations generally use the format "Author, Chapter/Title, Book Name/Publication, Page, Publisher/Date"

In Text Citations

References are not cited in text. Merely a number enclosed by brackets is used to indicate which document from the references section is used.
